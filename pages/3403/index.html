Admissions Information:

Applying to college is one of the more daunting tasks an individual goes through in their life, but it's also one of the most rewarding since it allows them to truly showcase their achievements and express their character. When students apply, they consider a variety of factors, some which are measurable and other that are based on things like individual experience which cannot be codified. For some of the measurable factors, though, a concise display can be useful to help students choose the right school for them.
